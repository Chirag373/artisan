Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 27, 2025

Welcome to ArtistaHub.com ("ArtistaHub," "we," "our," "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our website and related services.

By accessing ArtistaHub, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please stop using the website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

A. Information You Provide to Us

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Artist profile details
  • Business descriptions and bio
  • Uploaded artwork/images
  • Links to external websites (Etsy, Shopify, etc.)
  • Subscription/payment information (processed through Stripe)
  • Messages sent to us (support inquiries, feedback, etc.)

We do not store your full payment details. All payment information is securely processed by Stripe.

B. Automatically Collected Information

When you use our site, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on pages
  • Referral links
  • Cookies and tracking data

This helps us improve website performance and security.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage artist profiles
  • Display your profile and content on ArtistaHub
  • Improve SEO indexing for your profile
  • Personalize user experience
  • Communicate updates, notices, and support messages
  • Process subscription payments
  • Improve website performance and usability
  • Prevent fraud, misuse, or unauthorized access

3. How Your Information Is Shared

We value your privacy. We never sell your information.

Your information may be shared only with:

A. Service Providers

We may use third-party partners to operate our platform, such as:

  • Stripe (payment processing)
  • Analytics providers (Google Analytics or similar)
  • Hosting providers
  • Email sending platforms

These partners only receive the information necessary to perform their services.

B. Publicly Visible Information

If you create an artist profile, information you choose to display may be public, including:

  • Profile name
  • Bio
  • Artwork or product images
  • Links to your external shops
  • Social media handles
  • Contact email (if you choose to provide it)

C. Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

ArtistaHub uses cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Improve website performance
  • Analyze traffic and usage patterns
  • Enhance SEO and search visibility

You may disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not work properly.

5. Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce agreements

If you delete your account, we remove or anonymize personal data unless required to retain it for legal or security reasons.

6. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted communication (HTTPS)
  • Secure servers
  • Restricted access controls
  • Stripe-secured payment handling

However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection. You use the site at your own risk.

7. Your Choices & Rights

You may:

  • Update your profile information
  • Change or remove profile visibility
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Opt out of marketing emails
  • Disable cookies

To make a request, contact us at support@ArtistaHub.com.

8. Children's Privacy

ArtistaHub is not intended for individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Third-Party Links

Artist profiles may contain links to external websites such as:

  • Etsy
  • Shopify
  • TikTok
  • Instagram
  • Personal websites

We do not control or endorse external sites and are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes take effect once posted on this page. Continued use of ArtistaHub means you accept these updated terms.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us at:

Email: support@ArtistaHub.com
Website: www.ArtistaHub.com
